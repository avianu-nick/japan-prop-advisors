FAQ

Common Questions

Can foreigners really own property in Japan?

Yes! Unlike many Asian countries, Japan allows foreign nationals to purchase real estate with the same rights as Japanese citizens. There are no restrictions on land or property ownership.

How much money do I need to invest?

Properties in Japan can be surprisingly affordable. We've helped clients purchase homes starting from just $20,000 USD. Including renovation and management setup, most clients invest between $30,000-$100,000 USD.

What kind of returns can I expect?

Our properties typically generate 10-15% annual returns through vacation rentals or long-term tenants. Many clients recoup their initial investment within 24-36 months.

Do I need to speak Japanese or visit Japan?

No! Our service is designed specifically for remote investors. We handle all communication, translate documents, and coordinate with local partners. While visiting your property is always welcome, it's not required.
